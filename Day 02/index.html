Recipe: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients: 1 cup butter 1 cup white sugar 2 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon baking soda 2 cups chocolate chips
Instructions: Preheat your oven to 350°F. Cream together the butter and sugar until smooth. Gradually mix in the flour and baking soda. Stir in the chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
